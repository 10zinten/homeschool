Start Your School

Thanks for signing up for Homeschool App!

I know from my own home life that managing a child's school schedule is a lot of work. I've worked with my wife who homeschools our children to develop this application.

This application is the system that we use to keep our school life sane. I'm glad I can share it, and I hope the app can bring some sanity to you as well.

Setting up for school takes work so we'll walk you through the process of starting your school year over the next few pages.

I don't expect you to do all of this at once because I recognize that building a schedule takes time. This walkthrough equips you with the skills to start, then you're free to build out your plans for the student or students in your home.
